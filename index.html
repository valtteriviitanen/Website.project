Welcome to Real Madrid – The Pride of Football

Our Legacy

Real Madrid, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful football clubs in the world. With a rich history of achievements, including 35 La Liga titles and 14 UEFA Champions League trophies, Real Madrid has established itself as a global symbol of excellence.

Achievements

  • 35 La Liga Titles
  • 14 UEFA Champions League Titles
  • 19 Copa del Rey Trophies
  • 5 FIFA Club World Cup Wins

Legendary Players

Throughout its history, Real Madrid has been home to some of the greatest players the world has ever seen:

  • Alfredo Di Stéfano
  • Cristiano Ronaldo
  • Zinedine Zidane
  • Raúl González
  • Ferenc Puskás

Our Rivalries

Real Madrid has fierce rivalries with teams like Barcelona, Atlético Madrid, and Valencia. The encounters with Barcelona, known as "El Clásico," are legendary and are among the most watched football matches worldwide.
